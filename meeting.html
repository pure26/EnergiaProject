Skype For Business

Meetings

Managing Meetings

Skype for Business lets you conduct audio, video, and web conferencing on the go. You can easily plan an online meeting well in advance, in addition to hosting one in real time. As many as 250 people can join a meeting with their individual devices.

Schedule a Meeting

Schedule a Meeting in Outlook

Skype for Business can be easily integrated with the Outlook plugin. This allows you to schedule your online meetings through Outlook with a single click. The presenter can choose a meeting topic and can also determine which participants can join the meeting. The details and information for a particular meeting or conference call are available on the Outlook template.

  • Open your Outlook Calendar, click the Home tab, and click New Skype Meeting.
  • Complete the meeting request just like you normally would.

Set Meeting Options

You can set some meeting options in Outlook before you even send the meeting request, like who will be able to get into the meeting directly and who waits in the virtual lobby.

Manage Meeting Participants

Meeting getting unruly? If you are the presenter, you are in control.

  • Click the Participants button user group to open the Participants pane so you can see a list of everyone in the meeting.
  • Click the Participant Actions button.
  • Click one or multiple buttons to apply these settings to all attendees.

Join a Meeting

This feature allows employees to join your meeting with a single click. If the meeting was scheduled well in advance, participants can join conveniently from their calendar meeting reminders.

After joining the meeting, the conversation window shown below will appear. This window shows the number of participating members and allows them to chat with each other. Clicking on the number of participants will show a list of them.

At any point during the meeting you may invite more individuals by clicking on the Invite More People button at the top-right corner of the conversation window.

Outlook Conversation History

  • You can see all your Skype for Business activity from your Outlook window by clicking on Conversation History from the left panel.
  • As with your regular inbox mail, your Skype activity is grouped by date and arranged from most to least recent.
  • Your history includes past conversations, meetings, calls made/received, and any other Skype activity.
