Accessing Skype

If Skype for Business is not already running, launch it by opening the start menu, typing in “skype”, and selecting Skype for Business 2016

In the sign-in window below, enter your Aramco email address (Firstname.Lastname) into the Sign-in address field, as shown. Wait for the sign-in process to complete, and the main Skype for Business interface should appear.
